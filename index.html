Although most people think that oranges, lemons or other citrus are considered to have a lot of vitamin C in them, think about other fruits...like, chillies! Did you know that chillies have about 107mg (milligrams) of vitamin C in it? Oranges actually only have 69mg!

A chili actually has hotter parts in it than others. Do you want to know the hottest? Well, the part of the chili that is closest to the stem is the hottest because it is the place with the most capsaicin.

Did you know that Saffron is the most expensive spice in the world? This is because the spice is so rare and hard to collect since you have to collect strands from the flower Crocus sativus. You have to harvest the flowers by hand and de-thread the saffron from the flower by hand which as you can imagine, a lot of work.

Tumeric is classified as one of the most healthiest spices in the world. This is because it is good for the mind & body, helps to relieve asthma symptoms and can provide anti-cancer effects.
