The Hottest Spice

You will not believe the hottest spice in the world! It is called Cayenne Pepper reaching on the Scoville Scale a total of 50,000 units! It's a type of Capsicum Annum Chili species and is frequently used in dishes. It had also been used in traditional medicines.

The Hottest Spice

Althought you might think that the Carolina Reaper or Trinidad Morgua Scorpion might win the title of the hottest chili. But another chili has broken the record. It's Dragon's Breath with 2.48 million units on the Scoville Scale! It's even said that it could kill you because it's so hot!
