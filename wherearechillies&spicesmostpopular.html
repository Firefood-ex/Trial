Where are Chillies & Spices Most Popular?

Spices

Spices are mostly used in tropical countries. Countries that use the most spices are Ethiopia, India & Indonesia.

Ethiopia

Ethiopia Commonly uses 6 main spices: Berbere spice, Mitmita, Awaze, Timiz, Niter Kibbeh and Meklesha which are all in order from most popular. All of these spices are essential to Ethiopian Cusine.

India

If there is any country that uses the most spices, it's India. India uses many spices but there is 3 most common: Tumeric, Cardamom and Cumin. These spices are all used for spicy Indian dishes and Cumin was actually used for mummification!

Indonesia

Indonesia uses spices including Java Tamarin (used for food), Lemongrass (used for curry and tea), and Nutmeg (used for a traditional food called semur). But of course, they use a lot more especially since their dishes require lots of spices to flavour the food.

Chillies

Many countries consume chillies and the country that eats the most consumes half of the world's chillies! The biggest chilli consuming countries are China, Turkey and Indonesia.

China

China eats 50% of the world's chillies and yes, this is the country that consumes half of the world's chillies globally.

Turkey

Turkey eats 6% of the world's chillies consuming about 2,108 thousand tonnes!

Indonesia

Indonesia, like Turkey, eats 6% of the world's chillies. They consume approximately 1,971 thousand tonnes of chilli!
