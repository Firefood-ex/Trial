First Spices

Spices date back many years ago, and you'll be surprised. Some of the most oldest spices are commonly used today. These first 3 spices were either discovered in India or Asia. Here they are:

1. Herbs

Herbs date back to the year 2,980BC. The first written record was created on clay tablets by the Sumerians, Southern Mesopotamia 5,000 years ago.

2. Cinnamon

Cinnamon dates back to the year 28000BC. Back then, cinnamon was so valuable it was equal in worth to gold & ivory.

3. Black Pepper

Black pepper dates back to the year 1500BC. It was mostly used in India and was both a seasoning for food and medicine.
